Catalan Culture

In this part of the website I want to share all the bits of my culture that I like, anything from food to TV series. I hope this serves as a door for anyone interested in this culture to learn more!

TV Series & Radio Podcasts

The best TV series that Catalan TV has ever produced is Plats Bruts. The show is a sitcom that follows the stupid day to day events of two friends, David Güell and Josep Lopes. The show can be watched on YouTube, here, and in TV3 website.

Another series that I enjoy a lot is Bricoheroes. Partially aired on TV but with all the episodes on YouTube, each episode of the show consists of Jair Domínguez and Peyu going over some DIY build. They are both humorists, so expect to have a laugh with them!

Another show that Jair and Peyu have is El búnquer. A radio podcast that they do with Néus, talking about the biography of a famous person in history, random facts about towns and cities of Catalonia and answering questions that people send. It is all recorded in Youtube, and also in audio only in spotify and other podcast streaming services.
